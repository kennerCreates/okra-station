# Okra Station — Game Design

## Overview
2D isometric real-time strategy / tower defense hybrid.

## Players & Roles
- **Multiplayer:** Two players
  - **Robotic Horde (attacker):** Controls a swarm of robots with a single objective — destroy the main town center
  - **Nature Defender (tower defense):** Builds defenses and units to protect the town center

## Units
- Each player has **1 hero unit** under direct player control
- All other units are **AI-controlled**

## Robotic Horde
- Single-minded attack on the main town center building
- Uses **flow field navigation**

## Nature Defender
- Tower defense mechanics protecting the town center

## Maps
- **Procedurally generated**
